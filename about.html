Dary Barclay

Re-recording Mixer + Sound Designer

I’m a re-recording mixer and sound designer based in Calgary, Alberta.

I’ve worked professionally in audio for about 12 years, specializing in post-production for film, TV, commercials, video games, and immersive VR/AR. I gravitate toward projects built around strong storytelling and enjoy exploring how sound can deepen the narrative. Technically minded but equally focused on creativity, I make sure audio supports the story without overshadowing it.

Along the way, I’ve helped design and build three studios: a George Massenburg–style joint live/mix room, a dedicated 7.1 surround space, and my current home studio, which is evolving into a 7.1.4 Dolby Atmos environment.

When I’m not working, you'll find me hanging out with my kids, biking around the city, checking out a local brewery, on the drums, or planning my next outdoor trip - camping, hiking, or paddling.
